A Pigeon's Path to Peace

- Wings of Freedom -

Background Story


You're a street pigeon just minding your own business until one day you open the newspaper and read the following:

"The symbol of the dove of peace has nothing in common with a street pigeon, as it is estimated that street pigeons are involved in up to 2000 fights a year."

Enraged, you throw the newspaper into the corner and swear to yourself:
"I will prove to the world that I can go a year without a fight!

How to Play


Use the arrow keys "up" and "down" to avoid other pigeons.

Don't get into a fight for 365 days.

The other pigeons will increase in speed.

"Your Level" increases each time you have avoided 10 fights.

Your movement speed will increase with each Level Up.

You Made it!

After you survived the last day of the year without a fight, you glide to the ground, exhausted but happy. In the landing approach, your last forces dwindle and your eyes go black.
Suddenly you see a small light at the end of the horizon. It comes closer to you until it finally envelops you completely and a loud voice booms:
 "You have proven yourself worthy! From now on you shall live as a symbol of peace in paradise among your equals."
With newfound strength you see that your once so dirty gray plumage shines in glistening white and you think to yourself:
"I am Pigeon the White!"
